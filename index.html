Privacy Policy

Arrow Escape Puzzle
Last Updated: November 10, 2025
Summary: Arrow Escape Puzzle is a completely offline puzzle game that does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected as all gameplay data remains on your device.

1. Information Collection

Arrow Escape Puzzle does not collect any personal information from its users. The app operates entirely offline and does not require:

  • User accounts or registration
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Internet connection or network access

2. Local Data Storage

The app may store the following data locally on your device for gameplay purposes:

  • Current puzzle progress
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Difficulty level selections

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

3. Permissions

Arrow Escape Puzzle does not request any special permissions from your device. The app requires only the basic permissions necessary for any Android application to function.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services

5. Children's Privacy

Arrow Escape Puzzle is suitable for users of all ages. Since the app does not collect any personal information, there are no specific privacy concerns related to children's use of the application.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features. No data is transmitted over the internet or stored on external servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. If the app adds features that involve data collection in the future, we will update this policy accordingly and notify users through the app.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall Arrow Escape Puzzle from your device. This will remove all locally stored game progress and settings.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the app's local data through your device settings.
